About

Hey Buddy! aims at synchronizing your contacts from various online connectivity websites such as Facebook, Gmail, Yahoo, LinkedIn, Twitter, etc. It enables you to import your contacts, reach out to your contacts and keep a record of the last time you reached out to any contact. You would be able to set up automated reminders to help you stay in touch and follow-up with people in a timely manner. It is an easy-to-use interface for regular communicators.
